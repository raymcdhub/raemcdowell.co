Case Study · 01

Property listings, automated

A student accommodation agency was spending hours manually copying and pasting social media posts for every new listing (and old ones) and deciding when to post them. The process was inconsistent, time-consuming, and entirely dependent on whoever had the bandwidth that week.

I built a pipeline where a new property listing automatically triggers an AI rewrite — optimised for social — and schedules it to go live twice a week without anyone touching it.

The result: consistent social presence, zero manual effort, and a content calendar that runs itself.

Case Study · 02

Lead qualification on Daft

A homeshare facilitator receives a high volume of accommodation enquiries through Daft.ie every day. Each enquiry triggers an email notification to a shared inbox, so someone on the team had to open each one individually, copy a standard response template, paste it into a reply, and send it manually.

With approximately 50 enquiries arriving per day across two staff members, that process was consuming close to two hours of working time every single day on a task that required zero judgement and delivered no additional value to the applicant.

I delivered an automated flow that now handles enquiry responses end-to-end: new messages from Daft.ie trigger the workflow, which extracts the enquirer's details, pulls a branded reply template, sends a personalised acknowledgement, and keeps the inbox clean automatically.

The result is a process that no longer consumes staff time and actively improves the quality of leads reaching the team. Every Daft.ie enquiry now triggers an automated response containing a qualification form, ensuring that only genuinely interested prospects who complete it progress further. The team's attention is directed where it matters most. Based on approximately 50 daily enquiries at around one minute each, the automation recovers an estimated two hours per day previously spent on manual replies. Time now available for higher-value work, including following up with pre-qualified leads. No vendor dependency, no additional licence cost, and no reliance on systems the team cannot see or control.
