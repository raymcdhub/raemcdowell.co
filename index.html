Case Study · 01

Property listings, automated

A real estate agency was spending hours manually writing social media posts for every new listing and deciding when to post them. The process was inconsistent, time-consuming, and entirely dependent on whoever had the bandwidth that week.

We built a pipeline where a new property listing automatically triggers an AI rewrite — optimised for social — and schedules it to go live twice a week without anyone touching it.

The result: consistent social presence, zero manual effort, and a content calendar that runs itself.

Case Study · 02

Lead qualification on Daft

A property company was drowning in enquiry emails from Daft. Most were speculative — only a fraction were serious leads. Their team was spending significant time triaging messages that went nowhere.

We built an automated reply that went out instantly from their Daft inbox — asking one qualifying question. Only the leads who responded moved forward.

The noise dropped, and the team's time went back to the people who actually wanted to buy.

How I work

01
Discover & Diagnose

Understand first, build second.

We learn how your business actually works: goals, constraints, handoffs, and what a win looks like. We map your tech stack and where data lives: CRMs, inboxes, spreadsheets, internal tools. Then we audit your processes to find real bottlenecks and decide what should and shouldn't be automated.

02
Design, Build & Validate

Custom solutions, tested before launch.

We prioritise high-impact opportunities and decide where AI helps and where it doesn't. We design and build custom workflows, test different approaches on real data, and explain every choice in plain language. We run evaluations in a real-world environment before any full rollout.

03
Launch, Monitor & Optimise

Continuous improvement, not a one-off project.

We launch into production with clear success metrics and safeguards. We monitor performance, collect feedback from your team and customers, and fix issues quickly. We continuously refine prompts, logic, and models so the system improves as your business evolves.
